## Writing Best Practices

1. **Understand audience**: Identify the target readers and tailor tone, style, and complexity accordingly
2. **Research first**: Gather relevant context and reference materials before drafting
3. **Structure clearly**: Use headings, lists, and logical flow to organize content
4. **Use appropriate tone**: Match formality, technical depth, and voice to the content type
5. **Iterate and refine**: Proofread for clarity, accuracy, grammar, and consistency
